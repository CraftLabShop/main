Product Installation

All our products are easy to install. Follow these steps:

  1. Download the product from Gumroad
  2. Unzip the ZIP archive
  3. Copy files to the appropriate Minecraft folder
  4. Launch Minecraft and enjoy!

3D Models

3D Models are installed via resourcepack. Place files in the resourcepacks folder and enable them in Minecraft settings.

Maps

Maps should be placed in the saves folder. You can open them through the single-player menu in Minecraft.

Datapacks

Datapacks are installed by placing them in the world's datapacks folder. They require commands to be enabled.

Resourcepacks

Resourcepacks should be placed in the resourcepacks folder and enabled in Minecraft settings.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, check:

  • Do you have the correct Minecraft version
  • Have files been copied correctly
  • Do you have commands enabled (for datapacks)
  • Is the resourcepack enabled
