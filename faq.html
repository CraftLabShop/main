FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install a purchased product?

All our products include detailed installation instructions in the README file. Generally, just copy the files to the appropriate Minecraft folder and launch the game.

Are products compatible with my Minecraft version?

All products are marked with Minecraft versions they are compatible with. Check the product description before purchasing.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Check our refund policy for details.

Can I use products on servers?

Yes, all our products can be used in both single-player and on servers, as long as they don't violate Minecraft's terms of use.

Do products contain viruses?

No, all our products are safe and regularly scanned for viruses. We pride ourselves on the quality of our software.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us via email: support@craftlab.com or through our Discord. We usually respond within 24 hours.
