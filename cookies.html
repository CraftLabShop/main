Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

What cookies do we use?

Essential cookies

Required for the website to function properly:

  • Language preferences
  • Currency preferences
  • Session state

Analytics cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our website:

  • Number of visits
  • Popular pages
  • Time spent on site

Managing cookies

You can manage cookie settings in your browser. Note that disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-party cookies

We use third-party services (e.g., Gumroad for payments) that may set their own cookies.

How long do we store cookies?

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies are stored for 30 days.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.
